INDIAN POLITICAL PARTIES

BJP

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) was established in 1980. This party is the successor to the Bharatiya Jana Sangh founded by Syama Prasad Mukherjee in 1951.

INC

The Indian National Congress (INC) is a political party in India. It was founded in 1885 by Allan Octavian Hume, a former British civil servant.

BJD

The “Tall Man” Shri. Biju Patnaik. The brave and great son of Odisha and India who is acknowledged across India and abroad, from Odisha to Indonesia, as a man who stood for the development and progress of the people of Odisha. He is the very breath for the BJD which gives us life and strength.

AAP

The Aam Aadmi Party is a political party in India. It was founded on 26 November 2012 by Arvind Kejriwal and his then-companions,following the 2011 Indian anti-corruption movement against then Indian government of Indian National Congress.

BSP

The Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) is a political party in India that was formed to represent Bahujans (literally means "community in majority"), referring to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes (OBC), along with religious minorities

RJD

The Rashtriya Janata Dal is an Indian political party, based in the states of Bihar, Jharkhand and Kerala. The party was founded in 1997 by Lalu Prasad Yadav. The party's support base has traditionally been Other Backward Classes, Dalits and Muslims and it is considered a political champion of the lower castes.
